Our Farming Practices

Macadamia Farming

Our macadamia nuts are cultivated with care, ensuring a balance between sustainability and high yields. They are rich in nutrients, offering exceptional flavor and quality for both local and international markets.

  • Rich in healthy fats and antioxidants.
  • Grown using organic methods.
  • Hand-picked to ensure only the best quality reaches you.

Irrigated Arrowroots

Our irrigated arrowroots thrive with consistent water supply, ensuring optimal growth and exceptional quality. These gluten-free crops are perfect for health-conscious diets.

  • Rich in carbohydrates and fiber.
  • Carefully harvested for freshness.
  • Ideal for balanced meals.

Boma Rhodes Farming

Our Boma Rhodes grass provides excellent forage for livestock, boosting both their health and productivity. Grown with sustainable methods, this grass ensures environmental and agricultural benefits.

  • Highly nutritious fodder for livestock.
  • Supports soil conservation and reduces erosion.
  • Harvested at the optimal stage for maximum quality.
